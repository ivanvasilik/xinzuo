# XINZUO Knife Series - Verified Manufacturer Specifications

## Document Purpose
Official manufacturer specifications for all 4 knife series. Use these as the source of truth for all copywriting.

---

**Blade Material:** 67 Layers Damascus with 10Cr15CoMoV Steel  
**Handle Material:** Oak Burnt Handle  
**Hardness:** 60±1 HRC  
**Heat Treatment:** Vacuum Heat Treatment and Deep Freeze  

**Positioning:** Premium Damascus with VG10-equivalent core

## LAN SERIES

**Blade Material:** 73 Layers Damascus Steel with 14Cr14MoVNb Powder Steel  
**Handle Material:** Olive Wood  
**Hardness:** 62±2 HRC  
**Heat Treatment:** Vacuum Heat Treatment and Deep Freeze  

**Positioning:** Flagship powder steel Damascus (highest performance)

## RETRO SERIES

**Blade Material:** 3 Layers 10Cr15CoMoV Composite Steel  
**Handle Material:** Red Wood + Black Buffalo Horn

## SUPREME SERIES

**Blade Material:** German 1.4116 Steel

## PRICING HIERARCHY (CONFIRMED)

1. **Supreme** - Entry ($72) - German 1.4116
2. **Retro** - Mid - 10Cr15CoMoV 3-layer
3. **Mo** - Premium - 10Cr15CoMoV + 67 Damascus
4. **Lan** - Flagship - 14Cr14MoVNb powder + 73 Damascus

---

## KEY DIFFERENTIATORS

### Steel Types:
- **Supreme:** Single-layer German 1.4116 (softest, easiest to sharpen)
- **Retro:** 3-layer 10Cr15CoMoV composite (VG10-equivalent performance, budget price)
- **Mo:** 10Cr15CoMoV core + 67 Damascus layers (same performance as Retro, premium aesthetics)
- **Lan:** 14Cr14MoVNb powder steel + 73 Damascus (hardest, sharpest, flagship)

### Hardness Progression:
- **Supreme:** 56-58 HRC (easiest to maintain)
- **Retro:** 60±1 HRC (professional-grade)
- **Mo:** 60±1 HRC (same as Retro)
- **Lan:** 62±2 HRC (extreme edge retention)

### Damascus Layering:
- **Supreme:** None (single-layer)
- **Retro:** None (3-layer composite, not full Damascus)
- **Mo:** 67 layers
- **Lan:** 73 layers (most intricate pattern)

### Heat Treatment:
- **Supreme, Retro, Lan:** Vacuum heat treatment
- **Mo, Lan:** Vacuum + Deep Freeze (cryo treatment)

---

## NOTES FOR COPYWRITING

1. **10Cr15CoMoV = VG10-equivalent** - Safe to use this comparison (similar composition)
2. **Retro vs Mo:** Same core steel and hardness, but Mo has Damascus cladding (visual upgrade)
3. **Lan is unique:** Only series with powder steel (14Cr14MoVNb) - this is the flagship differentiator
4. **Supreme positioning:** Entry-level but German quality (not cheap Chinese steel)
